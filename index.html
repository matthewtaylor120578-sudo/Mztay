01 · About Dyno Nobel

180 years.
Six continents.
One legacy.

Dyno Nobel (ASX: DNL) is a global leader in commercial explosives, blasting systems and mining services. With a history dating back 180 years to the pioneering work of Alfred Nobel, Dyno Nobel services major mining and infrastructure customers across six continents.

Following the demerger of its fertilisers business and the rebrand from Incitec Pivot, Dyno Nobel is now a focused, independent ASX-listed entity with a clear mandate to accelerate operational excellence, innovation, and strategic growth.
