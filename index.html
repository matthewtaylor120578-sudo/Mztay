About us

A GLOBAL LEADER IN
COMMERCIAL EXPLOSIVES.

For more than 150 years, Dyno Nobel has helped customers move the earth that feeds industry — from iron ore and copper to coal, gold, and aggregates. We engineer initiation systems, manufacture explosives, and deliver blasting services that set the global benchmark for safety and performance.

We're part of one of the world's most trusted names in mining services — backed by deep R&D, manufacturing scale, and crews who know every pit wall they work on.

How it works

FROM APPLY TO DAY ONE.

A simple, respectful process. Run by P&C Partners on behalf of Dyno Nobel.

  1. 01

    Apply online

    30 seconds. CV + a few details. No cover letter required.

  2. 02

    Call with P&C

    A talent partner gets to know you — values, goals, what you're looking for beyond the role.

  3. 03

    Meet Dyno Nobel

    Technical and panel interviews with the team you'd actually be working with. Site visits where possible.

  4. 04

    Offer & onboarding

    Reference checks, offer, and a structured 30-60-90 plan supported by P&C through your first three months.
